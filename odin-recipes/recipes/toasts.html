Sunshine Toasts

Description

Oh geez, we're so toasted!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread;
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted;
  • 1 large egg;
  • salt to taste;

Steps

  1. Cut a circle in the center of bread with a cookie cutter. Brush melted butter on both sides of bread.
  2. Heat a small skillet over medium heat. Add bread and cook until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  3. Flip, then crack egg into the hole. Cook until egg is set on the bottom, about 1 minute; season with salt.
  4. Cover, reduce the heat to low, and cook until egg white is completely set, 3 to 4 minutes.
